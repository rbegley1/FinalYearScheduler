Welcome to our GAA Ticket Scheduler

Come and book your draw here first. Eliminate the competition from other clubs!

Step 1

First you need to navigate to the bookings section of the website to begin filling out the form.

Step 2

Next fill out your club details on the form and select your desired draw dates.

Step 3

On submit of a successful booking this message will pop-up.

Rules
  1. No past dates.
  2. End date must be after start date.
  3. Each club will have one record.
  4. A local draw will have a 20km radius.
  5. A grand draw will have a 50km radius.
  6. A club within these boundaries may have a draw booked on your dates.
  7. In this case new dates must be chosen.

Book your draw now!











Problem

  • There is too much competition between clubs selling tickets in the same areas.
  • This competition is resulting in less funds being raised by each club as more and more people will begin to turn ticket-sellers away.
  • These reasons make the idea of ticket-selling a more daunting experience, and less people are willing to volunteer their time for it.
  • Because of this fundraisers are less effective, but this competition can be eradicated.

Solution

  • This system is proposing that the sale of tickets within the GAA be organised using a schedule.
  • This will allow each club to have a fair chance in promoting their fundraiser by booking their dates through this system.
  • This will ensure there are no conflicts between neighbouring clubs and that each fundraiser can be run smoothly.
  • No more competition when selling tickets and more effective fundraisers will be the result.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to possible queries you may have. Feel free to get in touch if you need to know anything else.

Will my draw be booked as soon as I have submitted the request?

Yes, as long as you enter valid information and recieve the confirmation message with no errors then you can guarantee that your draw has been booked. Now all you need to do is wait to your dates and begin selling your tickets. Good luck!

Is there a way in which clubs can discuss the possibility of swapping draw dates.

At this current time there is no specific feature which allows this, but there is nothing stopping two clubs having a discussion about changing dates. If both clubs agree to the change just get in contact and the information can be changed.

How does the system calculate the draw distances?

The system uses the options given on the form to do this. If you choose to hold a local draw this will pass in the value of 20 kilometres to check for other club draws, and if you choose a grand draw this parameter will be set to 50 kilometres.

Can another club book on the same date as my club?

To put this simply, yes they can. If a club is outside of the boundary distance of your draw then they have the freedom to run their draw on the same dates. But if a club who are within the boundary of your draw try to book the same dates, then they will be asked to choose different dates.

Once I choose my clubs dates, can I change them if needs be?

Yes, if something comes up and you need to change the dates which your club are going to run your draw then just get in contact and we will change these details for you. If this is the case, please be sure that you wish to change dates because there is no guarantee the dates will still be available if you change your mind and we will not be held accountable for this.
